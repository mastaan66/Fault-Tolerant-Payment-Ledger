# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free project. Be respectful,
specific, and constructive. Critique ideas and code, not people. Assume good
intent while remaining precise about correctness, especially where financial
safety is involved.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, threats,
doxing, sexualized attention, sustained disruption, and publishing another
person's private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, commits, issues, and
other contributions that violate this standard. They may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer contact options on
their GitHub profile. Do not use a public issue for a sensitive report. Reports
will be reviewed as promptly and confidentially as practical. Anyone handling a
report must disclose conflicts of interest and recuse themselves when needed.

## Scope

This standard applies in project spaces and when someone is officially
representing the project in public spaces.
